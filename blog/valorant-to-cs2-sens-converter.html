How to Convert Valorant Sensitivity to CS2 (Perfect 1:1 Aim)

If you're moving from Riot's tactical shooter over to Valve's Source 2 engine, you know that keeping your muscle memory intact is the difference between ranking up and getting hard-stuck. Because both games use entirely different engines, a sensitivity of 0.5 in Valorant is not 0.5 in CS2.

The Magic Multiplier

To convert your Valorant sensitivity directly to Counter-Strike 2 (or Apex Legends), you need to use the magic multiplier: 3.181818.

  • The Formula: Valorant Sens × 3.181818 = CS2 Sens
  • Example: If you play on 0.4 in Valorant, your CS2 sensitivity should be 1.2727.

Remember that your mouse DPI stays exactly the same. Only the in-game sensitivity value changes.
