How to Convert Valorant Sensitivity to Rainbow Six Siege

Switching your main game from Valorant to Rainbow Six Siege? The worst thing you can do is guess your sensitivity. Both games run on entirely different engines with different field-of-view (FOV) scaling, meaning your muscle memory will be completely thrown off if you don't convert it mathematically.

Why You Must Convert Your Sens

Professional aimers rely on "cm/360" (the physical distance your mouse moves on the pad to perform a full 360-degree turn in-game). To maintain the exact same cm/360 from Valorant in Rainbow Six Siege, a specific multiplier must be applied to your raw sensitivity number.
